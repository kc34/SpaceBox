What is it

SpaceBox is a space simulation sandbox game, where players create and control the dynamics of stars, planets, and moons. The controls are simple and intuitive: - Click and drag to pan around - Up and down arrow keys to zoom in/out - Click and hold to create a moon, drag to give initial velocity - Hold for longer to create a planet, drag to give initial velocity - Hold for even longer to create a star, with increasing size
